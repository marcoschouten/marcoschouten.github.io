I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [DTU](https://www.dtu.dk/), where I am fortunate to be co-advised by [Dimitrios Papadopoulos](https://people.csail.mit.edu/dimpapa/) and [Serge Belongie](https://sergebelongie.github.io/).

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning, focusing on the personalization and controllability of diffusion models.

Prior to my PhD pursuit, I completed my Master's in Computer Science at [KTH](https://www.kth.se/) with a thesis on SLAM-based pose estimation of underwater vehicles advised by [John Folkesson](https://www.kth.se/profile/johnf). Earlier, I graduated from the [University of Genoa](https://unige.it/) with a BS in Computer Science and Engineering.
I worked on research and software engineering projects during my internships at [Aptiv](https://www.aptiv.com/) AI-core team.

Feel free to get in touch with me at marscho at dtu dot dk.
