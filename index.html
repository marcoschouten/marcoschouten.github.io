Marco Schouten

I am a Ph.D. student at DTU and a member of the Belongie Lab, I work under the supervision of Dim P. Papadopoulos and Prof. Serge Belongie.

I graduated from KTH in 2022 with an M.S. in machine learning, and from the University of Genoa in 2020 with a B.S. in computer science and engineering.

My research interests include diffusion and flow-based generative models, and self-supervised learning, with applications in controllable image synthesis and spatial understanding.

Outside of work, I enjoy making art.
