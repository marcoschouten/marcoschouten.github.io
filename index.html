Marco Schouten

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at DTU, Copenhagen, and a member of the Belongie Lab. I am fortunate to be advised by Dim P. Papadopoulos and Serge Belongie. I research on generative modeling and visual understanding with diffusion and large language models, focusing on controllable synthesis and spatial reasoning. Outside research, I make art.
