I am a PhD candidate at DTU and the Pioneer Centre for AI, advised by Prof. Dim P. Papadopoulos and Prof. Serge Belongie (Belongie Lab). My research focuses on generative modeling: controllable diffusion for image editing, structured scene understanding, and multimodal/VLM-guided editing.

Previously, research intern at Aptiv (HD map generation, autonomous driving) and the Swedish Maritime Robotics Centre (SLAM, underwater robotics).
