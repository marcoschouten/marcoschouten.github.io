I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) and research affiliate at the Pioneer Centre for AI. I am co-advised by Dim P. Papadopoulos and Serge Belongie. Previously, I was a research intern at Aptiv and TU Delft, earned my M.Sc. from KTH Royal Institute of Technology and my B.Sc. from the University of Genoa.

My research focuses on personalization and control of generative models in computer vision, with applications to image generation, and editing.
