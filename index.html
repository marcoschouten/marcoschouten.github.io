Marco Schouten

I am a Ph.D. student in the Visual Computing Department at DTU and a member of the Belongie Lab, working under the supervision of Dim P. Papadopoulos and Prof. Serge Belongie. I graduated from KTH in 2022 with an M.S. in machine learning and from the University of Genoa in 2020 with a B.S. in computer science and engineering. I am interested in diffusion and flow-based generative models and self-supervised learning for controllable image synthesis and spatial understanding. Outside of work, I enjoy making art and participating in triathlon sports.
