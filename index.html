I am a PhD candidate at DTU and the Pioneer Centre for AI, advised by Prof. Dim P. Papadopoulos and Prof. Serge Belongie (Belongie Lab). I build generative systems for visual content using diffusion models, and LLM Agents for controllable image generation and editing, and agentic visual reasoning, at the intersection of computer vision and graphics.
